Frequently Asked Question (FAQs) :
  1. Question: For how many papers can I take the GATE 2022 exam?
    Answer : Candidates are allowed to apply for only one paper of GATE 2022 on the basis of their educational qualification.
  2. Question: Can I expect any change in the GATE 2022 syllabus?
    Answer : The authorities will release the revised syllabus of GATE 2022 on the official website.
  3. Question: How do I apply Online for GATE 2022?
    Answer : You can visit the official website for the filling of the application form
    (a) There you will find the application form link.
    (b) Start filling your details in the application form after clicking on the link.
    (c) Now you can click on the payment gateway for the payment of the application form.
    (d) Download the pdf of the generated application form.
  4. Question: I have passed my B.Tech with 50%. Am I eligible for GATE 2022?
    Answer : Yes, you are eligible. But you will not be able to apply for some PSUs and IITs because they have criteria of 60% or more for the admissions.
  5. Question: How many times can I appear for GATE? What is the age limit?
    Answer : There are no age criteria for the GATE exam. You can appear as many attempts you can.
  6. Question: Any white paper is allowed for rough work or calculations?
    Answer : No. The white paper is not allowed. The candidates will be provided with a scribble pad for their calculations and they have to return it after the examination.
  7. Question: I have passed in 2016 but I have a backlog. Can I apply for GATE 2022?
    Answer : Yes, you can apply but you have to clear your backlog before August 2022.
