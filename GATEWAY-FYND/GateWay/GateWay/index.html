GATE 2023
  • IIT Kanpur is the Organizer of the 2023 Gate Exam.
  • The examination will be conducted over six days and twelve sessions on Friday 5th, Saturday 6th, Sunday 7th, Friday 12th, Saturday 13th and Sunday 14th of February 2023.

  • The exam will be Fully Computer Based Test (CBT).
